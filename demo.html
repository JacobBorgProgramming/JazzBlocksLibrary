Buttons "X,Y, and Z" have sub styles. These sub styles have different effects and colors called "mods". Mods always start with "_". "ButtonX_teal": buttonX calls the button type "X", while "_teal" calls the styling of the button. Just "buttonX" will call the default "X" button.

Button Mods

Blog NEW

buttonX supports mods "teal", "green", "blue" and "orange".

buttonY supports mods "teal" and "green". (buttonY cannot support orange because it is buttonY's default.)

buttonZ supports the mod "teal".

Remember to seperate classes (buttonX) and mods (teal) with "_". Example: buttonX_teal
