Learn to use this library

There are seven button classes, X, Y, Z, AA, BB, CC, and Neon

X, Y, and Z buttons can use "mods". Mods are seperated with "_".

Button mods are teal, blue, orange, yellow, red, pink, and green.

There are currently five paragraph classes pc, pf, po, ph, and pn.

pc

pf

po

ph

pn

Paragraph size mods are s, m, and l.

Paragraph style mods are u, o, and st. Underline=u, overline=o, and strike-through=st

When using multiple mods the order goes class_size_style.

You can mix and match mods, just keep the proper order.
